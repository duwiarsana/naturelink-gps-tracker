| **IMEI** | **8** | **01 23 45 67 89 10 11 22** | **BCD code, indicate:123456789101122** |
| **Data length** | **2** | **01 00** | **Little endian format, data length, This**<br><br>**example means 1 byte** |

| **Check sum** | **1** | **1C** | **Checksum for all the data contents** |
| **End** | **1** | **0A** | **Fixed End Bit** |
